🍳 How to cook food & elixirs

Steps
  1. Stand next to a lit cooking pot (stables, villages, enemy camps — or light your own with wood + any flame).
  2. Open inventory → Materials, highlight an item and choose Hold. Add up to 5 ingredients.
  3. Back out of the menu; near the pot press Cook to toss them in, then collect the dish or elixir.
Rules
  • Food: use ingredients that share one effect — more or higher-tier = stronger / longer.
  • Elixir: 1+ critter (bug, frog, lizard) + 1+ monster part. The critter sets the effect.
  • Don't mix two different effects — they cancel out (plain or Dubious result).
  • Don't mix food + monster parts → Dubious Food. Monster parts only belong with critters, for elixirs.
  • Add a Monster Extract to gamble level/duration; Dragon parts / Star Fragments greatly extend duration.
